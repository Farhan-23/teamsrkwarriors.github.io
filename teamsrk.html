KHAN'S FAMILY



Shah Rukh met Gauri for the first time in 1984 and they tied the knot post pursuing a breezy romance. She was born Gauri Chhibba. The marriage took place in 1991, much before SRK became the legendary star of Bollywood. Gauri had extended all the support to her actor husband and presently shares chief responsibilities at Red Chillies Entertainment, a film distribution and production entity set by SRK. Through thick and thin, she stood by her husband and contributed to his stupendous success.

Aryan Khan
Born- November 13, 1997
Aryan Khan, eldest son of Shah Rukh and Gauri Khan, made his acting debut in his father’s blockbuster, ‘Kabhi Khushi Kabhi Gham’, which was directed by Karan Johar. He is interested in Taekwondo. He is presently undergoing higher studies in London’s Sevenoaks School.

Suhana Khan
Born-May 22, 2000
Suhana, the second child of Shah Rukh and Gauri Khan is somewhat media shy. The young girl is more interested in sports and dancing. She is currently studying at Dhirubhai Ambani International School in Mumbai. Unlike her elder brother, she has not made his silver screen debut yet. As per the latest news, she may also pursue higher studies in London like her brother Aryan.

AbRam Khan Born-May 2013 SRK and Gauri Khan had their third child Abram through surrogacy. The youngest son of the couple, Abram was born prematurely and spent a lot of time in hospital. The latest SRK flick- ‘Happy New Year’ features the child in the credit rolls along with his father.Unlike his grown up children, Shah Rukh prefers to keep the toddler away from media glare as far as possible.
